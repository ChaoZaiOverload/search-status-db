Privacy Policy

Extension: Douban Status Search
Last updated: 2026-04-27

What data we collect

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It does not contact any server other than www.douban.com.

Local storage

To avoid redundant network requests, the extension stores previously fetched Douban status post HTML (keyed by status ID) in chrome.storage.local on your device. This data never leaves your browser and can be deleted at any time using the "清缓存" (clear cache) button inside the extension.

Network requests

The extension fetches pages from https://www.douban.com/ using your existing browser session. No credentials or personal information are extracted or stored beyond the cached post HTML described above.

Third parties

No data is shared with any third party. No analytics, tracking, or advertising libraries are included.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/ChaoZaiOverload/search-status-db.
